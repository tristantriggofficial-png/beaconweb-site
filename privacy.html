Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

Effective Date: June 1, 2026 · Last Updated: June 1, 2026

BeaconWeb ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at beaconweb.co. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights — in plain English, not legalese.

This policy complies with the California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA) and applies to all visitors to our website.

What We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you fill out a contact form on our site, we collect:

  • Your name and business name
  • Your email address and phone number
  • Information about your project or business (whatever you choose to share)

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time on page
  • Referring website or search query
  • General geographic location (city/region, not precise location)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This is collected via Google Analytics. We do not collect precise geolocation data, and we do not sell this data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide quotes
  • Communicate about projects you've engaged us for
  • Send occasional updates about our services (you can opt out anytime)
  • Analyze website traffic to improve our site
  • Manage our client relationships through our CRM system (GoHighLevel)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Analytics — Website traffic and behavior tracking. Google's Privacy Policy
  • GoHighLevel (CRM) — Contact and project management. Form submissions go to our GoHighLevel account. GoHighLevel's Privacy Policy
  • Netlify — Website hosting. Netlify's Privacy Policy

Cookies

Our website uses cookies primarily through Google Analytics. These are analytics cookies that help us understand how visitors use our site. They do not track you across other websites.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use our website.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and client information for as long as we have an active business relationship with you, plus a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping purposes. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

California Residents — Your Rights (CalOPPA)

As a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tristantrigg@beaconweb.co. We will respond within 45 days.

Do Not Track

Our website does not currently respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals from browsers. We use Google Analytics which may track your behavior across our site.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Our site uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
