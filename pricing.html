Pricing FAQ

Common questions about cost and setup.

What's included in the setup fee?+
The setup fee covers building your entire system from scratch — GoHighLevel account configuration, all automation workflows, qualifying message copy, follow-up sequences, calendar integration, CRM pipeline setup, and end-to-end testing. It's a one-time build fee. You're not paying for templates; you're paying for a system built specifically for your business.
What does the monthly fee cover?+
Monthly management includes monitoring all automations, making adjustments as needed, optimizing sequences based on response rates, adding or updating workflows, and monthly check-in calls. It also covers your GoHighLevel platform access — you don't need a separate subscription.
Is there a contract?+
No long-term contract. Month-to-month with 30 days' notice to cancel. We keep clients by delivering results, not by locking them in. If the system isn't working for you, you shouldn't have to stay.
What do I own if I cancel?+
You own the GoHighLevel account and all the automations we built. If you cancel, you keep everything — the workflows, the contacts, the pipeline, the calendar integrations. You can continue running it yourself or with another provider. Nothing is held hostage.
How long does setup take?+
Most systems are live within 7–14 days of kickoff. Timeline depends on how quickly we can get your business information, phone number setup, and calendar connected. We've built systems in 5 days when clients move fast.
Can I upgrade tiers later?+
Yes, anytime. If you start on Core and want to add AI qualification and follow-up sequences, we build those onto your existing system. The upgrade fee is the difference in setup cost between tiers, and your monthly rate adjusts going forward.
Do I need to already use GoHighLevel?+
No. We set it up for you. If you already have a GHL account, we can build inside it. If you don't, we set one up as part of your system. Your monthly management fee includes access to the platform.
Are there any other costs?+
SMS sending has a small per-message cost through GoHighLevel's carrier — typically a fraction of a cent per message. For most businesses this runs $10–$30/month depending on lead volume. We'll give you a realistic estimate during the discovery call based on your numbers.
