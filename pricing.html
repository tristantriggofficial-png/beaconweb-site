Why One System

We don't sell tiers. Here's why.

A lot of agencies sell "starter packages" — a few automations that feel like a deal but leave the biggest gaps open. We've seen what happens when a business has instant response but no follow-up, or follow-up but no missed call text-back. Partial automation creates false confidence. You think you're covered, but you're still losing leads at the exact moments the system should be catching them.

The Lead-to-Appointment System is designed as a complete loop. Every piece depends on the others to work. Skipping the follow-up sequences means leads who don't respond to the first message go cold — even though 60% of closed deals happen after the 4th contact. Skipping the review request means you're doing the work but not compounding the reputation. Partial systems produce partial results.

So we install the whole thing. You get every automation. And you pay one flat rate — no tiered confusion, no upgrading later to get the pieces you actually needed from day one.

Common Questions

What people ask before they commit.

What's included in the setup fee?+
The setup fee covers building your entire system from scratch — GoHighLevel account configuration, all 8 automation workflows, qualifying message copy, follow-up sequences, calendar integration, CRM pipeline setup, and end-to-end testing. It's a one-time build fee. You're not paying for templates — you're paying for a system built specifically for your business.
What does the monthly fee cover?+
Monthly management includes monitoring all automations, making adjustments as needed, optimizing sequences based on response rates, adding or updating workflows, and a monthly check-in call to review performance. It also covers your GoHighLevel platform access — you don't need a separate GHL subscription.
Is there a long-term contract?+
No. Month-to-month with 30 days' notice to cancel. We keep clients by delivering results, not by locking them in. If the system isn't working for you, you shouldn't be forced to stay.
What do I keep if I cancel?+
Everything. The GoHighLevel account is yours — all contacts, conversation history, workflows, pipeline data, and automations stay with you. You can continue running it yourself or hand it to another provider. Nothing is held back.
How long does setup take?+
7–14 days for most businesses. The main variable is A2P carrier registration (3–7 days on the carrier's end). We build the full system in parallel while registration processes, so we're ready to go live the moment it clears.
Are there any other costs?+
SMS sending has a small per-message cost through GoHighLevel's carrier — typically a fraction of a cent per message. For most businesses this runs $10–$30/month depending on lead volume. We'll estimate your specific costs on the discovery call based on your actual lead numbers.
Do I need to already use GoHighLevel?+
No. We set it up for you as part of the build. If you already have a GHL account, we build inside your existing account. If not, we create one — your monthly fee covers platform access either way.
