The System

We Turn Automotive Leads Into Booked Appointments in Under 60 Seconds.

Eight automations wired together. Every inquiry gets a response before a competitor can pick up the phone. Every missed call gets followed up. Every completed job gets a review request. Automatically.

The 8 Automations

Every piece of the system, explained.

These aren't tools you log into. They run in the background, 24 hours a day, turning inquiries into appointments while you focus on the work.

01
📥
Lead Capture
Every form, every ad, every channel — captured in one place automatically.

Whether a lead comes in through your website form, a Facebook ad, Google, or a referral link — it all flows into one unified inbox. No more checking multiple platforms. No more leads falling through the cracks because someone submitted a form you didn't see. Every inquiry lands in the same place, tagged, timestamped, and ready to be acted on.

02
⚡
Instant SMS Response
Leads get a text back in under 60 seconds. No more losing deals to slow follow-up.

The moment a lead submits any form or inquiry, they get a personalized text message — automatically. No delay, no manual action required. This single automation wins more business than almost anything else, because 78% of customers hire whoever responds first. While your competitors are still sleeping, your system has already made contact and started the conversation.

03
🤖
AI Qualification
Automated replies filter serious buyers from tire-kickers before you ever pick up the phone.

After the initial response, the system sends a short qualifying question — asking about timeline, budget, or specific needs depending on your business. The replies tell you immediately who's serious and who's just browsing. You only spend time on leads who've already confirmed they're ready to move. The system handles the rest and either continues the conversation or politely closes the loop.

04
🔁
Follow-Up Sequences
7 days of automated touches that keep working a lead until they respond.

Most leads don't respond to the first message. That doesn't mean they're not interested — life gets in the way. The system sends a pre-built series of follow-up messages over 7 days via SMS, email, or both. It stops automatically the moment they reply or book. No awkward manual follow-ups. No wondering if you're being annoying. The system handles the persistence so you don't have to.

05
📞
Missed Call Text-Back
Every missed call gets an instant text. No lead goes cold because you couldn't pick up.

Someone calls and you're on a job, in a meeting, or just unavailable. Before automation, that call went to voicemail and maybe got called back later — maybe not. Now, the moment a call is missed, your system sends them a text: "Hey, sorry I missed you — what can I help you with?" They respond. The conversation starts. The job is still winnable. This one automation recovers a meaningful percentage of leads that would have otherwise gone cold.

06
📅
Calendar Booking
Leads book themselves directly into your calendar — no back and forth.

Once a lead is qualified, the system sends them a direct booking link. They pick a time that works for them. It appears on your calendar. No email chains, no phone tag, no scheduling assistant needed. You show up to confirmed appointments instead of chasing people down. This is where the "appointment" part of Lead-to-Appointment actually happens — and it happens without you being involved in the scheduling at all.

07
📊
Pipeline Tracking
See exactly where every lead stands, at a glance, in real time.

Every lead moves through a visual pipeline — New Lead → Contacted → Qualified → Appointment Booked → Job Complete. You open the dashboard and see exactly who's where with no digging required. When something needs your attention, it's obvious. When the system is working, you can see it. This is how you go from "I think we have some leads somewhere" to complete clarity on your business's sales pipeline.

08
⭐
Review Requests
Every completed job triggers an automatic request for a 5-star review.

When a job is marked complete, the system automatically sends the customer a review request via SMS. It's personal, it's timely, and it goes out at exactly the right moment — when the customer is still happy about the work. Most businesses know they should ask for reviews but forget in the rush of daily operations. This makes forgetting impossible. More reviews mean better rankings, more trust, and more leads coming in to start the cycle again.

Our Process

From kickoff to running system — five steps.

We do the heavy lifting. You review, approve, and then the system runs. Most businesses are fully live in under two weeks.

1
We map your current process

Free discovery call. We learn how leads come in, how you follow up today, where appointments happen, and what tools you're already using. No guesswork — we design around your actual business.

2
We build the automation in GoHighLevel

All automations are built in GoHighLevel — the same platform trusted by thousands of agencies. We set up your pipeline, build your sequences, configure the missed call text-back, connect your calendar, and wire up your review requests.

3
We test every touchpoint

Before anything goes live, we test the entire system end-to-end. Submit a test lead, trigger a missed call, go through the qualification flow, book a test appointment. We don't hand over something we haven't run through ourselves.

4
It runs — leads become appointments automatically

System goes live. From this point, every new lead gets an instant response. Every missed call gets a text. Every follow-up runs on schedule. Every completed job gets a review request. You do nothing — it just works.

5
Monthly check-ins to optimize

We review response rates, booking rates, and review counts every month. If something can be improved — better qualifying questions, adjusted follow-up timing, new automation triggers — we handle it. The system gets better over time.
