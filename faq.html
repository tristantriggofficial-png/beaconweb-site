FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about the Lead-to-Appointment System — how it works, what's involved, and what to expect.

General Questions
What exactly is the Lead-to-Appointment System?+
It's a complete automation built in GoHighLevel that handles everything that happens between "a lead comes in" and "a lead is on your calendar." That includes: instant SMS response, AI qualification replies, 7-day follow-up sequences, missed call text-back, calendar booking, pipeline tracking, and automated review requests after each job. All 8 pieces work together — you don't have to manage any of it.
How is this different from just buying GoHighLevel myself?+
GoHighLevel is a powerful platform — but it's also complex. Most businesses that buy it themselves spend weeks trying to set it up and end up with a half-configured system that doesn't actually run. We build it, test it, and hand you something that works on day one. Then we maintain it so you never have to dig into the settings again.
How quickly will I see results?+
The instant SMS response and missed call text-back kick in from day one — the moment a lead comes in or a call is missed, the system fires. Most clients notice a higher contact rate within the first week. The follow-up sequences and review requests compound over time as more leads flow through the system.
Do I need to be tech-savvy to use this?+
No. The system runs in the background. You'll have a mobile app (GoHighLevel's) where you can see your pipeline, respond to conversations, and check your calendar. Beyond that, you don't need to touch anything — the automations run themselves. If anything needs adjusting, you contact us and we handle it.
Can I see what the automations say before they go live?+
Yes, always. You review and approve every message before the system goes live. The initial SMS, every follow-up in the sequence, the missed call text-back message, the review request — we write them, you review them, and nothing sends until you sign off.
Technical Questions
What platform is this built on?+
GoHighLevel (also called GHL or HighLevel). It's the leading CRM and automation platform for service businesses, used by thousands of agencies and hundreds of thousands of businesses. It handles SMS, email, phone, calendar, pipeline, forms, and more — all in one place.
Does it work with my existing website?+
Yes. We either embed a new lead capture form on your existing site, or we connect your existing forms to GoHighLevel via webhook. Either way, lead data flows into the system automatically from the moment someone submits a form on your website.
What happens if a lead replies STOP?+
The system immediately and permanently removes them from all sequences. They receive no further automated messages. This is built into GoHighLevel's carrier-level compliance — it's automatic, instant, and legally required under TCPA regulations. You never have to manually manage opt-outs.
What happens if a lead is hostile or abusive in replies?+
The automated sequence will continue unless the lead uses an opt-out keyword (STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, etc.). You can manually pause or remove any contact from sequences at any time through the GHL mobile app or desktop. We also set up keyword triggers to flag unusual conversations for your review.
Does it work with my existing CRM or booking software?+
GoHighLevel integrates with most common tools via Zapier or native integration — Google Calendar, Calendly (though we replace it), ServiceTitan, Jobber, and more. We'll review your current stack on the discovery call. In most cases, GHL can either replace your existing booking tool or sync with it.
What phone number sends the texts?+
We register a dedicated business number for your system through GoHighLevel — typically a local area code number that matches your business location. This is distinct from your personal cell number. Leads text this number, and all replies come into your GHL inbox. You can also forward calls from this number to your main business line.
What about A2P 10DLC registration?+
A2P 10DLC is a carrier-level requirement for business SMS in the US — it verifies your business is legitimate before letting you send texts at scale. We handle the registration process for you as part of setup. It typically takes 3–7 business days to get approved, and nothing goes live until it's confirmed.
Who owns the data and the automations?+
You own everything. The GoHighLevel account is registered under your business. All contacts, conversation history, workflows, and pipeline data belong to you. If you ever leave BeaconWeb, you take the full account with you — we don't hold anything hostage.
Getting Started
What do you need from me to get started?+
After the discovery call: your business name and address (for A2P registration), a brief description of your services and ideal customer, your existing calendar or booking tool, and your website URL. That's usually enough to start building. We collect everything else through an onboarding form.
How long does setup take?+
7–14 days for most businesses. The main variable is A2P carrier registration, which takes 3–7 days on the carrier's end. We build the full system in parallel while registration processes, so we're ready to go live the moment it's approved.
Is there a contract?+
No long-term contract. We work month-to-month with 30 days' notice to cancel. A simple service agreement outlines the scope and terms — it's straightforward, not a 20-page document.
How do I know if this is the right fit before spending money?+
That's what the discovery call is for. Free, 20 minutes, no pitch. We look at how leads come in for your business, how you follow up today, and where the gaps are. If the system makes sense for you, we'll say so. If it doesn't, we'll say that too.
