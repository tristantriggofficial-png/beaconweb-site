FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about the free audit — what it covers, how it works, and what happens next.

The Audit
What does the free audit actually include?+
We review your website speed and mobile experience, your Google Business Profile, your local SEO rankings, your social media presence, how leads get followed up with, and — if you're running any — your paid ad performance. You get a plain-English breakdown of what's working and what isn't.
Is the audit really free? What's the catch?+
It's free. No catch. We review your online presence and tell you what we find. If it makes sense to work together afterward, we'll say so and send a proposal. If it doesn't, you still keep the audit.
How long does the audit take?+
Results typically come back within 48 hours of your strategy call. Some audits take a bit longer if a business has multiple locations or several ad accounts to review.
What if I only need one thing fixed, not everything?+
That's common, and it's fine. The audit still covers all six areas so you know the full picture, but the proposal only covers what actually needs fixing — we don't upsell you on things that are already working.
The Process
What happens after I fill out the form?+
You'll get an onboarding form within the hour so we know what to look at, then a link to schedule your strategy call. On that call we walk through the audit and, if it's a fit, what a proposal would look like.
What's the onboarding form for?+
A short set of questions about your business — services, service area, current website and social accounts, and what you're hoping to improve. It's sent automatically right after you submit the inquiry form, and takes a few minutes to fill out.
Do I have to get on a call?+
Yes — the strategy call is how we walk you through the audit findings in plain English and answer questions specific to your business. It's typically 20-30 minutes, and it's the same free, no-pressure conversation for everyone.
Am I obligated to hire BeaconWeb after the audit?+
No. There's no obligation at any point. The strategy call is not a sales pitch in disguise — it's a walkthrough of what we found. Pricing and next steps only come up if you want them to.
Working Together
Where does pricing get discussed?+
On the strategy call, after the audit — once we actually know what your business needs. Pricing depends on scope, so we don't publish flat packages on the website.
What services does BeaconWeb actually offer?+
Website design and development, local SEO, social media management, and Google & Meta ads — plus reporting so you can see what's actually happening. Which of those you need comes out of the audit, not a fixed package.
Do you only work with businesses in the Coachella Valley?+
The Coachella Valley is our home base and where most of our clients are, but the audit process works for any local service business. If you're outside the Valley, mention it in the form and we'll let you know if it's a fit.
Is there a contract, and can I leave if it's not working?+
Terms are covered in the proposal, which is specific to what we agree to build or manage for you. That's a conversation for the strategy call, not something we lock in before we've even talked.
