Why the Free Audit

I got tired of watching businesses buy the wrong thing.

I've spent years building websites, running ads, and managing social media for local businesses in the Coachella Valley — car washes, service shops, small brands. Over and over, I'd meet an owner who'd already spent money on a new website, or a few months of ad spend, and it hadn't moved the needle. Nobody had ever actually looked at their whole picture — site, Google listing, socials, ads, follow-up — and told them what was actually broken.

So that's where BeaconWeb starts now. Before I pitch anything, I audit everything. Website speed and mobile experience, your Google Business Profile, where you actually rank locally, your social presence, how leads get followed up with, and whether your ad spend is working. You get a plain-English breakdown, whether you hire me or not.

Most of the time, that audit turns into a working relationship. Sometimes it doesn't, and that's fine — you still walk away knowing more about your own online presence than you did before.
