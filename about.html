Years Building Websites for the Valley

Where this started

I've spent 3 years building websites, running ads, and managing social media for local businesses in the Coachella Valley — car washes, service shops, small brands.

Over and over, I'd meet an owner who'd already spent money on a new website, or a few months of ad spend, and it hadn't moved the needle. Nobody had ever actually looked at their whole picture — site, Google listing, socials, ads, follow-up — and told them what was actually broken.

Why the audit comes first

So that's where BeaconWeb starts now. Before I pitch anything, I audit everything: website speed and mobile experience, your Google Business Profile, where you actually rank locally, your social presence, how leads get followed up with, and whether your ad spend is working.

You get a plain-English breakdown, whether you hire me or not — you'll receive your audit whether you hire us or not.

What happens next

Most of the time, that audit turns into a working relationship, and I build and manage the fix every month. Sometimes it doesn't, and that's fine — you still walk away knowing more about your own online presence than you did before.
